Recording Studio in Belfast’s Cathedral Quarter

Founded in 2011 by close friends Graham and George, Half Bap Recording Studios is located in the heart of Belfast city centre. Tucked away on the cobbled streets of Commercial Court, the studio overlooks the vibrant and picturesque Cathedral Quarter.

Our bespoke facility features a spacious live room, control room, and vocal booth—each acoustically treated to deliver the highest quality sound. Combined with a diverse collection of instruments and professional audio gear, we’ve created a space designed to inspire. Whether you're recording your first demo or a full album, our relaxed and creative environment allows artists to perform at their best.
